Split System Heating and Cooling

If you are looking for an efficient, heating and cooling solution for a small home or apartment in Melbourne, a split system air conditioner is an excellent choice.

Combining heating and air conditioning in one, split systems provide a convenient solution for all your home heating and cooling needs. 

Consisting of just two separate units (a wall mounted evaporator unit indoors and a condenser unit installed outdoors), a split system is a simple, low fuss solution to keeping you in comfort year round. With no ducts needed - a split system air conditioner installation is a straight forward process.

For larger properties or to heat/cool multiple rooms, multi-head split systems are also available.

Multi-​Head Split System

Offering the  efficiency of a standard split system  with the added convenience of heating and cooling multiple rooms.

A popular choice for larger households but with limited outdoor space, a multi-head split system air conditioner is capable of giving independent control for each unit/room. Allowing optimal comfort for the whole family. A multi-head split system installation is similar to that of a split system, but attaches multiple indoor head units to a single outdoor unit (opposed to the single indoor unit offered with a standard split system). 

Save money and running cost by only heating/cooling the rooms you are actively using. A single multi-head split system outdoor unit can be connected to up to 9 indoor units making it a great solution for any sized home. Year round comfort made easy.

Ducted Refrigerated Air Conditioning

Using efficient inverter technology, ducted refrigerated air conditioning systems are your 1-stop solution for unobtrusive, whole home heating and cooling. 

Running off electricity and capable of both heating and cooling, ducted refrigerated systems are a popular choice if you're looking for; a system that isn't effected by humidity levels, that is sleek and unobtrusive in design and is easily zoned for efficient heating and cooling control.

A form of ducted air conditioning/heating, these units allow for total home heating and cooling, delivering uniform temperature throughout.

Gas Ducted Heating

An exceptionally popular option in Melbourne for home heating, gas ducted heating is one of the most cost-effective and efficient forms of heating available.

Making use of gas to heat air, gas ducted heating systems distribute warmth ​throughout the entire home through a system of ducts. A versatile solution, ducts are able to be installed in the roof or floor cavity.

For your convenience, AIRCOOL is also able to provide Add-On Cooling for ducted heating systems. Ensuring total comfort in all seasons. 

Seamless heating and cooling systems, perfect for a modern, Australian home.

Evaporative Cooling

With low installation and operation costs, evaporative cooling systems are a smart and ​affordable cooling solution.

Keeping Melbourne homes cool on warm days, an evaporative cooling system installation allows circulated, cool air to travel throughout your home.

Ceiling ducts are connected to a central evaporative cooler unit that is installed on the roof. Cost efficitve cooling that is cohesive with life - windows and doors can even be left open while it's in use!

VRF/VRV SYSTEM

VRV is short for Variable Refrigerant Volume. It is a system that is designed for commercial use due to its volume and efficiency.

A VRV system consists of a large outdoor unit that connects to and serves multiple indoor units. Each indoor unit uses an electronic liquid expansion valve to control its refrigerant supply to match the demand of the space it serves. Making it exceptionally efficient.

The outdoor unit also varies its output to match the demands of the indoor units as needed. Ideal for hospitality venues and alike.

ADD-ON COOLING

Love your current gas ducted system, or looking at a new gas ducted heating installation but worried about how you'll stay cool in the warmer months? Fear not!

Add-on cooling is a form of cooling that can be installed alongside new or existing gas ducted heating systems to utilise the same ducts and vents. Please note: existing gas ducted heating systems may need to be updated or replaced to be compatible with refrigerated add-on cooling.
